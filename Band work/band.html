SKYBOSH

Introducing SkyBosh; a band formed by two brothers Andrew and James who had a dream, a dream that has nothing to do with forming a band. Originally from Kent, Andrew (the ugly one) moved up to Leeds to pursue his dream of being a world class dispenser only to upstaged by mighty MO. So that didn't happen so he decided to focus on music. Honestly the band would be more successful without Andrew but hey family is family, is something James would probably say.
